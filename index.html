Hello, I am Jack Boland. I am a UX/UI designer based in Texas. I hope to help as many users as possible with their online experiences. I see countless areas of improvement all the time, and to think that some people are having their days ruined by these imperfections makes me happy to help when I can. I believe my work can make a positive impact on the users that I come across whilst designing.

In 2022, I graduated with an Associate's in Video Game Development. In my time spent learning, I also noticed the many intricacies of UX/UI and its importance in creating a unique game play experience for the players. Some key skills I have learned through game development include prioritizing user enjoyment, finding unique solutions to unique problems, and creating an aesthetic that fits the narrative as well as product testing.

I love the idea of using my game development background and the learning of UX\UI design for the deveolpment of new apps and websites particularly with gaming.

Why UX/UI? While pursuing my video game development degree, I learned that I really enjoy programming and unique problem solving. I have come to immensely enjoy finding out ways in which an experience can be improved as well as seeing the work come to life. I enjoy creating a more efficient and pleasant experience for any person who happens to come across my work. The program with UT at Austin was a great opportunity to move into a more User friendly experience.
